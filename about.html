Our Story

Founded in 2021, SMEN International Company Limited emerged with a bold vision: to become the world's leading diversified conglomerate, serving multiple sectors with unmatched expertise and dedication.

From our humble beginnings, we recognized the immense potential in creating synergies across Contracting, Logistics, Real Estate, and Agriculture. This integrated approach allows us to deliver comprehensive solutions that address the complex challenges of modern business.

Today, we stand as a testament to what vision, hard work, and strategic thinking can achieve. With operations spanning multiple continents and a portfolio of successful projects, we continue to push boundaries and set new standards of excellence in everything we do.
